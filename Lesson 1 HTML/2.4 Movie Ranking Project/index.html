The Best Korean Drama Series According to Kenneth

My Top 3 Korean Drama Series of all time.

Reply 1988

I love this movie because it has a complete recipe of a typical Korean Drama with a Genre of RomCom. I love their bond they created since they were young and how they have each others backs when circumstances happens.

Vincenzo

This Series is a combination if a badass Mafia that is also a Lawyer which is very interesting. He faced the big company in a trial and performed Mafia things in order to shaken their company to get what he wanted.

It's okay to not be okay

This Drama shows how would you respond to person with mental instabilty and how to take care of them. I love how the characters portrayed in this drama. This is such a masterpiece.
